IN CLINIC

As a smart clinic, EVE Healthcare Centers offers a range of products designed to enhance the healthcare experience for both patients and medical professionals. Our products are carefully selected to ensure they are of the highest quality and are designed to improve efficiency, accuracy, and patient outcomes.


Some of our products include:

1. Patient portal: Our patient portal allows patients to easily access their health records, request appointments, and communicate with their healthcare providers.

2. Electronic health records (EHR): Our EHR system streamlines patient care by allowing medical professionals to access patient information quickly and easily, reducing errors and improving communication between healthcare providers.

3. Telehealth: Our telehealth platform allows patients to connect with their healthcare providers remotely, improving access to care and reducing wait times.

4. Medical devices: We offer a range of medical devices, including blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, and pulse oximeters, to help patients manage chronic conditions at home.

5. Point-of-care testing: Our point-of-care testing devices allow medical professionals to obtain rapid diagnostic results, improving patient outcomes and reducing wait times.



At EVE Healthcare Centers, we are committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. By offering a range of high-quality products, we are able to enhance the healthcare experience for both patients and medical professionals.
